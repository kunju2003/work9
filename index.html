SEA

A sea is a large body of salt water. There are particular seas and the sea. The sea commonly refers to the World Ocean, the wider body of seawater. Particular seas are either marginal seas, second-order sections of the oceanic sea

A mountain is an elevated portion of the Earth's crust, generally with steep sides that show significant exposed bedrock.
